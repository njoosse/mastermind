C++ game to play Mastermind against, the pegs will be chosen randomly by the program.

Duplicates are enabled, up to 4 of the same type may be selected.

Each peg is input by the player one at a time, the program will print how many pegs are in the combination, 
and how many are matching location and colour, the player wins when they have all of the pegs in the correct order,
4 correct locations.
